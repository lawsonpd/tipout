How to add a link to your home page

Even though Tipout runs in a browser, that doesn't mean you have to type in 'tipoutapp.com' every time you want to use it!

To make things simpler, just add a link to Tipout to your home screen. Here's how:

(In this example we're using Google's Chrome browser on Android, but the same idea works for other browsers and iOS, too.)

First, open your browser and navigate to Tipoutapp.com. In the top right corner of the app, you'll see three stacked dots that mark the browser menu.

Open the menu and select "Add to Home Screen". (This is what it looks like in Chrome. Other browsers may differ slightly.)

Finally, name the link "Tipout" and select "Add".

That's it! Best wishes :)
