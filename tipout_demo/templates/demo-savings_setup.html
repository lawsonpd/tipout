Savings setup

You can set a percent of your income to save each month. This will be saved separate from your tips & paychecks and won't be factored into your daily & weekly budgets.

For example, suppose you choose to save 20% monthly. If you then enter $100 in tips, $20 will be recorded as "savings" and $80 will be recorded as spendable income that can go toward your expenses and expenditures.

If you don't want to have savings automatically deducted, you can manually add to your savings anytime you wish.
