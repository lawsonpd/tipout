Keep in mind:

Anytime you deposit money into savings, Tipout assumes that money has already been entered in the form of tips, paychecks, or other funds, so your balance (the amount of money you have not including savings) will decrease to reflect the deposit.

On the other hand, if you withdraw money from savings, your balance will increase, since that money is assumed to be spendable after it's withdrawn.
