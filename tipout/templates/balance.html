Your balance is the amount of money you currently have (not including savings). It is tracked from the date you signed up until the present and roughly equals your total income (tips, paychecks, & other funds) minus your total spending (expenses & expenditures).

If it doesn't seem correct, make sure you have all of your tips, paychecks, & other funds entered, as well as all of your expenditures & expenses.
