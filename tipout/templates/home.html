Features

Track your earnings

Enter the tips and paychecks you earn to build a personal earnings profile and see how much you've earned for the month and year. This data is used to build your budget so you know what you can spend each day, week, or month.

Keep up with expenses

Make a list of your monthly bills and make sure you save enough money to pay all of your recurring expenses.

Manage your spending

Use daily expenditures (like coffee, lunch, and dinner) to keep your daily budget in check. You'll always know what you have left to spend today and still be able to pay your bills tomorrow.
